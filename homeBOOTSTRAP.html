ABOUT

Depaul English Medium High School, Rahuri Factory

OUR SCHOOL

Each student who joins the De Paul School is accepted as a gift from God and is moulded accordingly. The school is co-educational and follows Maharashtra State Syllabus. The medium of instruction is English.

OUR HISTORY

The school was started in 1994 with a view to impart knowledge suited to the challenging needs of the time. It is owned by the Vincentian Mission Service Society, Ahmednagar.

OUR FACILITY

Our school provides modern classrooms, library, science and computer laboratories, smart learning facilities, sports areas and transportation services.

Vision

To provide quality education to children and youth at the lowest possible cost that facilitates them to evolve as responsible human beings of character, competence, conscience, compassion and commitment.

Mission

To transform lives and communities through learning and create a new generation with a difference for God and society.
